# Streamaze Discord Bot

The Streamaze Discord bot currently has one main features. More features will be coming soon, please contact us with your Discord bot suggestions!

## Connect the Bot

You can invite the bot to your server [using this link](https://discord.com/api/oauth2/authorize?client_id=1058511637662941335&permissions=3136&scope=bot). Once the bot is in your server, you can use the features provided.

## Features

### Update Ticker Widget

The Streamaze Discord bot can update your [ticker widget](/widgets/ticker) with a custom message. By using `/t` or `/ticker` followed by your message, the bot will update your ticker widget with your message. For example, `/t Hello World!` will update your ticker widget with "Hello World!"
