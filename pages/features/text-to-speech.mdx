# Text-to-Speech

Play donation messages in a custom voice straight from the Dashboard. You can choose from any of the Streamlabs voices for free, or, with an ElevenLabs subscription you can enter your API key to choose an AI cloned voice. You can learn how to [connect ElevenLabs to your Dashboard here](/connections/elevenlabs).

Text-to-Speech requires the donation features which are only available to [Streamaze Subscribers or Premium](https://my.streamaze.live/account/upgrade) users.
